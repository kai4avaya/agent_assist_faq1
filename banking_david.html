Beyond Banking FAQ

Q: What services does Beyond Banking offer?

A: Beyond Banking offers a comprehensive range of banking services including personal banking, business banking, investment services, loans, and credit cards. We aim to provide innovative solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Q: How can I open a new account with Beyond Banking?

A: You can open a new account with Beyond Banking in several ways. Visit our website at www.beyondbanking.com and click on the "Open Account" button to apply online. Alternatively, you can visit any of our branch locations, where our friendly staff will guide you through the account opening process. Be sure to have your valid government-issued ID and other required documents ready.

Q: Does Beyond Banking have online and mobile banking options?

A: Yes, Beyond Banking offers secure online and mobile banking services. With our user-friendly online banking platform and mobile app, you can conveniently manage your accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, and access a range of other features from anywhere, at any time.

Q: What are the fees associated with Beyond Banking accounts?

A: At Beyond Banking, we believe in transparency and strive to keep our fees as low as possible. Our fees vary depending on the type of account and services you choose. You can find detailed information about our account fees, transaction fees, and other charges on our website or by speaking with a representative at your nearest branch.

Q: How can I apply for a loan or credit card with Beyond Banking?

A: Beyond Banking offers a variety of loan and credit card options to meet your financial needs. You can apply for a loan or credit card online by visiting our website and completing the application form. Alternatively, you can visit any of our branch locations, and our loan officers will be happy to assist you with the application process and provide you with personalized guidance.

Q: What security measures does Beyond Banking have in place to protect my information?

A: At Beyond Banking, the security and privacy of our customers' information is our top priority. We employ industry-leading security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and multi-factor authentication, to safeguard your personal and financial data. Additionally, we have strict policies and procedures in place to ensure the responsible handling of your information.

Q: How can I report a lost or stolen debit/credit card?

A: If your Beyond Banking debit or credit card is lost or stolen, please contact us immediately by calling our 24/7 customer service hotline at 1-800-BEYOND-BANK (1-800-123-6632). Our representatives will assist you in deactivating your card and taking the necessary steps to protect your account from unauthorized access.

Q: Does Beyond Banking offer investment services?

A: Yes, Beyond Banking provides comprehensive investment services through our dedicated investment advisory team. Our experienced financial advisors can help you develop personalized investment strategies aligned with your goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon. Whether you're planning for retirement, saving for education, or seeking long-term growth, we have a range of investment products and services to meet your needs.

Q: What are Beyond Banking's hours of operation?

A: Most of our branch locations are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. However, hours may vary by location, so we recommend checking our website or contacting your local branch for specific hours of operation. Our online and mobile banking services are available 24/7 for your convenience.

Q: How can I get in touch with Beyond Banking customer service?

A: You can reach Beyond Banking's customer service team through various channels. For general inquiries or assistance, you can call our customer service hotline at 1-800-BEYOND-BANK (1-800-123-6632), which is available 24/7. You can also send us an email at customerservice@beyondbanking.com, or visit any of our branch locations to speak with a representative in person.
